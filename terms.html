1. Introduction

Welcome to Codenprofit ("we," "our," or "us"). By accessing or using our website, platform, and services (collectively, the "Platform"), you agree to be bound by these Terms and Conditions ("Terms"). Codenprofit is an elite marketplace designed to bridge the gap between student talent and real-world business impact.

If you do not agree to these Terms, you may not access or use the Platform. Our goal is to maintain a high-trust environment where code becomes commercial capital without the interference of traditional agency markups.

2. Definitions

To ensure clarity within our ecosystem, we define the following roles and terms:

  • "Host" (Client): Refers to business owners, entrepreneurs, or entities who post projects, manage brand assets, and fund development milestones.
  • "Developer" (Architect): Refers to the verified student developers, engineers, and designers admitted into our network who provide services to Hosts.
  • "Project": A specific scope of work (e.g., Luxury Web Design, E-Commerce Build) submitted by a Host.
  • "Commercial Capital": The earnings generated by Developers upon the successful completion of milestones.
  • "Badges": Visual credentials awarded to Developers based on skill and performance (e.g., "UI Master").

3. Account Registration & Security

3.1 Eligibility

Hosts: Must be a legal business entity or an individual capable of forming a binding contract. You represent that you have the authority to represent your business.

Developers: Must be a verified student or recent graduate. Admission to the "Elite 150" network is subject to our rigorous vetting process, ensuring only the top 5% of applicants enter the marketplace.

3.2 Account Security

You are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of your login credentials (via Google, GitHub, or Email/Password). You agree to notify us immediately of any unauthorized access. Codenprofit is not liable for losses due to compromised accounts.

4. The "Badge & Strike" System

Codenprofit operates on a merit-based system to ensure luxury-grade quality across all deliverables.

4.1 Badges

Developers earn Badges (e.g., "ML Expert," "UI Master") based on successful project delivery. These Badges determine eligibility for higher-budget projects. Badges cannot be bought; they must be earned through consistent technical excellence.

Strike 1 (Warning)

Late delivery without notice or minor quality issues in code or design.

Strike 2 (Probation)

Unprofessional communication or missed major milestones. Account restricted to low-budget projects.

Strike 3 (Termination)

Abandonment of a project or severe violation of code standards. Results in permanent removal.

5. Services for Hosts (Clients)

5.1 Project Submission

Hosts must provide accurate details regarding their Vision, Industry, and Budget during the intake process. Misleading project descriptions may result in immediate cancellation without refund of the processing fee.

5.2 Milestones & Approval

Development is tracked via our proprietary Project Pulse system. Approval of a milestone constitutes absolute acceptance of the work and triggers the release of funds from escrow. Once a milestone is approved, it cannot be reversed.

5.3 Technical Guardianship

Hosts may opt for "Technical Guardianship," a subscription service for 24/7 maintenance. Cancellation of this service results in the termination of ongoing security updates and monitoring.

6. Services for Developers (Architects)

6.1 Project Claims

Claiming a project via the Marketplace is a binding professional commitment. Developers must adhere to the timeline specified at the point of claiming. Failure to do so impacts your Strike Meter.

6.2 Earnings & Payouts

Funds are only released to your withdrawable balance after the Host approves the specific milestone via the Pulse tracker. Codenprofit takes 0% Agency Markup, ensuring the developer receives 100% of the project value minus standard processing fees.

7. Payments & Refund Policy

Escrow Protection: To protect both parties, funds are held in a secure escrow account managed by Codenprofit. This ensures the Host has the funds and the Developer gets paid upon success.

Refunds: Hosts are entitled to a refund for uncompleted milestones if the Developer fails to deliver. Funds for milestones that have been approved and delivered are non-refundable.

8. Intellectual Property (IP) Rights

Upon Payment: Once a Host pays for a project in full, 100% of the Intellectual Property rights (code, design, digital assets) transfer immediately to the Host.

Portfolio Rights: Developers retain the right to display the finished work in their Codenprofit Portfolio for demonstration purposes, unless a specific Non-Disclosure Agreement (NDA) is signed prior to the project start.

9. Prohibited Conduct

Users agree NOT to attempt Circumvention: Taking payments or communications off the Codenprofit platform to avoid platform accountability is grounds for an immediate permanent ban. We also enforce a zero-tolerance policy for Plagiarism.
