1. Introduction

At Codenprofit ("we," "our," or "us"), we value the trust you place in our elite marketplace. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of the personal and business information you share with us. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you access our Platform.

By using Codenprofit, you agree to the data practices described in this policy. We take pride in building a secure bridge where digital transformation happens with absolute data integrity.

2. Information We Collect

We collect specific information depending on whether you join as a Host (Client) or a Developer (Architect).

2.1 For Hosts (Business Owners)

  • Identity Information: Business name, contact person, email, and phone number.
  • Brand Assets: Logos, color palettes, font files, and brand guidelines uploaded to the "Identity Hub".
  • Project Details: Proprietary business ideas, vision statements, industry targets, and budget ranges submitted via the Project Intake Portal.
  • Billing Information: Payment history and subscription details for "Technical Guardianship" plans.

2.2 For Developers (Students/Architects)

  • Professional Profile: Name, portfolio website links, and technical skills.
  • Verification Data: Information used to vet your "Top 5%" status, including academic history or past project links.
  • Performance Metrics: Data related to your "Badges" earned, "Strike" history, and success rates.
  • Financial Data: Banking or UPI details required to process "Commercial Capital" payouts.

2.3 Authentication Data

We use Firebase Authentication to secure your login. We collect login credentials including Email/Password or tokens from third-party providers like Google or GitHub to ensure a seamless and protected entry point.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to power the "Code to Profit" cycle and ensure the highest efficiency of our marketplace:

  • Elite Matchmaking: Using requirements and Developer Badges to suggest the perfect Architect.
  • Project Pulse Tracking: Storing milestone progress for real-time transparency.
  • Financial Processing: Managing escrow funds and milestone releases safely.
  • Communication: Facilitating the 24/7 "Connection Bridge" between you and our HR Leads.
  • Security & Compliance: Enforcing our "Strike Policy" and preventing unauthorized access.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell your personal data. We share information only in strictly necessary scenarios:

  • Between Host & Developer: Vision details and contact info are shared once a project is claimed to enable collaboration.
  • Service Providers: Trusted third-party vendors like Firebase, Stripe, and Cloud Hosting Providers.
  • Legal Requirements: Disclosure if required by law or to protect user safety and platform rights.

5. Data Security

We implement luxury-grade security measures to protect your digital assets and earnings:

Encryption

All data transmitted is protected using SSL/TLS protocols.

Secure Storage

Assets are stored using Google Firebase enterprise-grade infrastructure.

Access Control

Sensitive financial data is restricted to the account owner and authorized systems.

6. Your Rights

You maintain control over your data within the Codenprofit ecosystem:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correction: Update your Profile, Brand Assets, or Portfolio links via your Dashboard.
  • Deletion: Request account deletion (transaction records may be kept for legal compliance).

7. Intellectual Property & Confidentiality

Hosts: We acknowledge your "Vision" and "Project Details" are proprietary. We do not share these details publicly unless you choose the "Open Marketplace" setting.

Developers: Code submissions to the "Upload Portal" remain confidential until milestone approval and IP transfer to the Host.

8. Changes to This Policy

Codenprofit reserves the right to update this Privacy Policy. Significant changes will be announced via the Platform dashboard or Email notifications. Your continued use constitutes acceptance of these updates.
